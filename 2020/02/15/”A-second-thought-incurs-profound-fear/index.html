A second thought incurs profound fear

Question:

There were 100 people on an island, with five red eyes and 95 blue eyes. The island has three strange religious rules.

  1. They can’t look in the mirror and see the color of their eyes.

  2. They can’t tell each other what color their eyes are.

  3. Once someone knows he has red eyes, he must commit suicide that night.

One day a traveler came to the island. Because do not know the rules here, so he and the whole island people together when the carnival, accidentally said a word: you here have red eyes.
Question: given that the people on this island are smart enough to make rigorous logical reasoning, what happens on the island?

Analysis process:

First of all, I felt a little confused, because I could not reason rationally according to the visual test conditions. Then I read the answer immediately, but I still felt it was a little incredible. So I began to reason from the simplest one, and finally understood the answer.

Condition: you have red eyes here

1 red eye +99 blue eyes

Day 1:
Red eye perspective: 0 red eyes +99 blue eyes, identify yourself as red eyes, commit suicide the first night
Blue eye perspective: 1 red eye +98 blue eyes. I don’t know if I have red eyes, but if I have red eyes, I won’t commit suicide on the first night
Result: 1 red eye commits suicide, 99 blue eyes are ok

Conclusion: when only one red eyes, the red eyes will be on the first night confirmed that he is the only red eyes, blue eyes will be on the first day later confirmed that the crowd is only one red eye, so the red eyes after suicide, suspicion (if he was red eyes) with blue eyes is over, and sure you are blue eyes (all blue eyes)
2 red eyes plus 98 blue eyes

Day 1:
Red eye perspective: 1 red eye +98 blue eyes, confirm 1 red eye, but do not confirm whether they are red eyes
Blue eye Angle: 2 red eyes +97 blue eyes identify 2 red eyes, but do not identify themselves as red eyes
Result: no suicide the first night

Day two: here begins the premise that everyone knows there is more than one red eye in the crowd
Red eye perspective: 1 red eye +98 blue eyes, confirming that there are only 2 red eyes in the crowd
Blue eye Angle: 2 red eyes +97 blue eyes
Result: two red eyes committed suicide on the second night

Conclusion: when only two red eyes, two red eyes will confirm that he’s on the second night red eyes, blue eyes will be on the evening of the next day after confirmed the crowd has two red eyes, so the red eyes after suicide, suspicion (if he was red eyes) with blue eyes is over, and sure you are blue eyes (all blue eyes)
3 red eyes +97 blue eyes

Day 1:
Red eye Angle: 2 red eyes +97 blue eyes, confirm 2 red eyes, but do not know whether they are red eyes
Blue eye Angle: 3 red eyes +96 blue eyes, confirming 3 red eyes, but not knowing whether they are red eyes
Result: no suicide the first night

Day two: here begins the premise that everyone knows there is more than one red eye in the crowd
Red eye Angle: 2 red eyes +97 blue eyes, confirm 2 red eyes, but do not know whether they are red eyes
Blue eye Angle: 3 red eyes +96 blue eyes, confirming 3 red eyes, but not knowing whether they are red eyes
Result: no suicide the next night

Day 3: here begins the premise that everyone knows there are more than two red eyes in the crowd
Red eye perspective: 2 red eyes +97 blue eyes, confirm 2 red eyes, know that they are red eyes
Blue eye Angle: 3 red eyes +96 blue eyes, confirming 3 red eyes, but not knowing whether they are red eyes
Result: the third night 3 red eyes commit suicide

Conclusion: when only three red eyes, three red eyes will be on the third night confirmed he is red eyes, blue eyes will be on the third day later confirmed the crowd has three red eye, so the red eyes after suicide, suspicion (if he was red eyes) with blue eyes is over, and sure you are blue eyes (all blue eyes)

When you have n red eyes in the crowd, 100-n blue eyes

Red eyes see n-1 red eyes, blue eyes see n red eyes (always blue eyes see 1 more red eyes)

Red eyes will commit suicide on the NTH night (suicide condition: no one commits suicide before the NTH night)

Blue eyes will commit suicide on the n+1 night (suicide condition: no one commits suicide before the n+1 night)

Final Results:
On the fifth night all five people with red eyes will kill themselves

My own feelings:

Sometimes saying nothing can lead to the best outcome. If the red-eyed and the blue-eyed are not to blame, who is?

Eventually they’ll kill the traveler first.
