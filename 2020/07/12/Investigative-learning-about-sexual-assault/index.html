10 Myths about sexual Assault:

(1) What is sexual assault
Sexual assault is the act of seeking stimulation to satisfy one’s sexual needs. Including indecency, insults, threats, etc., which are sexually related ACTS against the person’s will.

(2) There is no relationship between age and sexual assault
In Henan, the youngest victim was 75 years old and the oldest was 95 years old

(3) Everyone is a potential abuser
Sexual assault is not about sex, it’s about control and it’s about power
Many rapists are not spouseless, he is the conquest of more women as a pride in his heart.

(5) Males suffer greater trauma after sexual assault
Male rape is not included in the statutory scope of rape, and it is very difficult to collect evidence and file a case

(6) What you wear has nothing to do with sexual assault
The 2018 University of Kansas exhibition “What Did You Wear When You were Sexually Assaulted?”
It has nothing to do with what you wear. One of the philosophies of sexual assault is that it’s not the victim’s fault.

(7) In cases of minors. Sexual assault is responsible for more than 75 percent of the cases
In the United States, 20 percent of girls and at least 10 percent of boys are sexually assaulted before age 18.
In remote areas and left-behind children, the incidence of campus sexual assault is high.

(8) According to international statistics, 85 percent of sexual assaults are committed by acquaintances
Among children aged 0-12, 56 percent of sexual assaults were committed by his or her family, 15 percent were committed by neighbors, and only 17 percent were committed by strangers.

(9) The most serious change of a person after sexual assault will be the abuser or suicide
In extreme cases, the victim may become a sex worker in the future or even begin to perform such behavior herself

(10) What to do after being sexually assaulted
Leave physical evidence, do not go to bath, semen and other things to stay, underwear not oh yo throw away, body scars, trauma timely identification
Pay attention to the media and privacy protection, is likely to become a secondary harm to the parties
