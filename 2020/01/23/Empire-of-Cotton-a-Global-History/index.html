Empire of Cotton: a Global History

About the author ：Sven Beckett is the laird bell professor of American history at Harvard University, teaching modern capitalist political economy, American capitalist history, gilded age American history, labor history, and global capitalism. Beckett is also co-chair of the Harvard project on capitalism and the Weatherhead global history project. Beckett wrote extensively about the economic, social and political history of capitalism. He is a member of the council of American learned societies and a fellow of the John Simon Guggenheim memorial foundation.

About the book ：The book covers a wide range of topics, from the origins and development of cotton to production and marketing strategies
