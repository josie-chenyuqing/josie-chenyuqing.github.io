The commercialization of intangible cultural heritage

The commercialization of intangible cultural heritage

—I spent six days working with my team members to build a platform to help commercialize intangible cultural heritage.

The poster I drew:

This is our main products: gourd paintings, lanterns and masks

The English name of our company is Leran, which means happy in Chinese.

The market of intangible cultural heritage

  1. The blank spots
    Nowadays, there is a lack of intangible cultural heritage education in the market, and the young generation in China lacks practical experience in this field.
    The seriousness is that fewer and fewer people are willing to learn intangible cultural heritage, and the inheritance of technology is facing great problems.
    Lack of traditional culture and the combination of modern technology, the older generation of artisans stuck in a rut, lack of innovation.

2 . solutions:
Sale of intangible cultural heritage related courses and online classes are available

Online courses mainly teach theories, the historical development of intangible cultural heritage, cultural deposits and production skills, etc.

Offline classes focus on practice, allowing students to personally experience the production process. To realize the combination of handicrafts and modern technology of intangible cultural heritage.

Start classes by age group.

  1. For users:
    (1) Pupils and their parents
    (2）Children aged 6 to 16 years
    (3) Cultivate the love of traditional culture and the inheritor from childhood
  1. Market size:
    70% of stores and personnel are abroad, North America: one on the East or West coast of the United States, one on the West coast of Canada. Europe: Concentrated in London’s Chinatown
    China’s economy is developing rapidly and cultural exchanges have become increasingly frequent since the reform and opening up
    The number of overseas students is increasing gradually. Taking advantage of this market opportunity, we aspire to be the leading exporter of Chinese culture
    Policy: In line with the spirit and concept of inheriting traditional culture, carry out systematic cultural education. Carry forward The Chinese culture abroad, gather overseas students and Overseas Chinese. The government strongly supports the export of culture.

​ 5. Core competitiveness

​ Innovation, passion, authenticity
​ Grab the overseas market
​ The good faith company
​ Product: Chinese soul, world mind
​ The moat of competitive core force: turn of fortune, lead a team

Company philosophy:
Many modern cultural and creative product designs ignore the interpretation of traditional culture. Inheritance can not be simply understood as the inheritance of carriers, but more importantly, the continuation of intangible cultural heritage. So while embodies innovative spirit, Cu sichuan culture co., LTD., also pay attention to keep the core of the intangible process, and attention to overseas Chinatown.

In the offline experience store, we will sell some modern products and handmade gift boxes made by intangible cultural heritage techniques. The gift box includes a simple tutorial of intangible cultural heritage products and raw materials for the products, allowing buyers to make ancient art in the far East with their own hands. For example, in the paper-cut gift box, we will provide a detailed paper-cut course and cultural science for people to learn, as well as the materials for making paper-cut.
