The new retail

Off line and online linkage promotion mode

  1. Live streaming of mainstream e-commerce
    (1) big V: KOL （Key opinion leader）
    Li jiaqi, ouyang nana
    With more accurate product information, it is generally possible to improve the credibility of its recommendation by shopping around, which has a great impact on the purchasing behavior of relevant groups
    Advantages: it is easy to be accepted and has a wider audience. You can put the live scene on the big screen in the store, so that people can notice it
    Disadvantages: if the leap itself quality does not pass, may be used as a stepping stone to other brands of shoes on top
    (2) close to life: KOC（key opinion consumer）
    University or high school campus flower campus grass, student President
    Consumers who can influence their friends and fans
    Just need some posters as the setting to start the propaganda and live broadcasting (activities can be conducted directly on campus), which is quick and close to daily life, so that students can be more easily attracted
    Advantages: cheap, can be sponsored by the student union within the school publicity, (sports) so that students can experience, more real
    Disadvantages: influence may not be as strong as KOL

  1. Game animation IP joint
    You can draw a game (king of glory, royal war, yimonghui) or a cartoon (naruto, Lord of one piece, FGO) on the side of the shoe in order to attract customers’ attention
    You can also add some out-of-print gift bags to the game, and you can only get the invitation code to redeem the game gift bag if you buy the shoes. Because the shoes themselves are not expensive, the game krypton gold phenomenon is serious, there will be a lot of collectors to buy shoes to exchange things.

  2. The SNS share
    Social network structure established through interpersonal relationship, thus carrying out viral marketing activities
    Feiyue can establish its own product group on the social platform, use social network to hold online activities and interactions, increase users’ experience by means like voting, survey coupons and questions, and spread feiyue brand awareness more quickly

  3. Interaction and feedback within small programs
    Pay is a member, all scan code into leap public number users can have the identity of a member, in the next use process can be priority to get brand activities, price push information. We will also improve and optimize the program and products through the feedback and evaluation of these users.
    Membership data: multi-dimensional data analysis
    Member management: according to the customer portrait, better define the consumer attributes, what kind of requirements
    Finally achieve the effect of double passenger flow
