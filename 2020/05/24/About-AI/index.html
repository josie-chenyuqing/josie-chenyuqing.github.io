About AI

I think freedom is a profound word. A lot of people do. Their childhood is the most free, but the truth is that people have never been free, they need to do a lot of things, need to follow the rules at all times, according to the established procedures. Similarly, students and employees have become the victims of surveillance and failed education under the evolving air system.
In fact, the purpose of using AI to monitor students’ learning is good. Parents and teachers hope to make students more obedient in this way, improve students’ learning efficiency, and enable students to get better grades so as to obtain a better future. It’s true, but it never occurred to them that it destroyed the students’ ability to have fun, deprived them of the joy they should have had at this age, and forced them to put on a false mask and live like robots. Sure, they may do well in the future, they will become excellent people, but they have lost their creativity forever, lost the joyful power to laugh freely, and they are all prisoners. Sink in the cage of the classroom.
For them, youth is a distant word of beauty.

However, to some extent, the AI monitoring system not only fails to help them, but also distorts their psychology. They can feel the intense pressure all the time, and they have to learn. They go to school not just for the sake of their own interests, but for the sake of a future job, a job they don’t even know what it is. This kind of learning, though efficient, is undoubtedly useless. They may go to jobs they hate and do things they don’t like in the future, but they have no choice, or they have no choice at all.
It’s not just a school problem, it’s a society problem. It’s like amazon spying on employees. It seems that what society wants is only machines that can work for it and contribute all their strength to its process, rather than living human beings. Human beings are called human beings because we have a variety of emotions, we feel sad and angry, we do whatever we want to do, we think and think.
And what is a machine? It’s emotionless, it’s fake, it’s not real. He will act according to the procedures given to him, not out of his own will. There is a big difference between the two. If the AI system is to continue to develop and enter every household, the situation will be deteriorated. Who knows if what you do will end up in some file? If it goes on like this, there will be no further development of human beings, because all their sense of innovation has been completely wiped out.
